How it works
Three steps to instant feedback

No setup required. Select your case, paste your note, and get a detailed report in seconds.

1
Select your clinical case

Choose from 10 NBME USMLE Step 2 standardized patient cases. Each case has a defined set of clinical concepts the history should cover.

2
Paste your patient note

Write or paste your patient history note as you would in a clinical encounter. The system accepts free-text — no special formatting required.

3
Review your feedback

See which concepts were documented with evidence highlighted in the note, which are missing, and accepted phrasings to improve your documentation.
